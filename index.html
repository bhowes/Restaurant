Our Story

WHEN WE MOVED FROM FLORENCE, ITALY in 1997, we were so excited to share with Denver authentic Italian food. In 1998, we opened a small market with imported Italian goods and a small deli menu. This was a time before the term “foodie” was coined and we sure received funny looks trying to sell burrata, bresaola, alici or mozzarella di bufala… but our customers realized that we offered things that weren’t around town. Now many of these items seem to be intrinsic to every trendy restaurant around town and we quietly enjoy the idea of being the first to introduce so many of these delicacies to Denver
