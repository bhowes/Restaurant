  • Community Table

    March 26 | 6:30pm

    Our multi-course, family-style meal on select Wednesday nights. Next Community Table: Wednesday, March 26 @ 6:30pm… introducing the “APERI-CENA” Come join us for a different kind of community event, described in detail below, with a huge buffet of Tuscan delicacies. Some of the dishes we will be offering: POLPETTINE DI CINGHIALE ~ wild boar meatballs CROSTINI MISTI TOSCANI ~ Tuscan toasts w/ mixed toppings FRITTATINE AL TARTUFO ~ Truffled fritters FOCACCINE RIPIENE ~ mini calzone FRITTO MISTO ~ lightly fried seafood VERDURE RIPIENE ~ stuffed vegetables among many other selections…. What is an Aperi-Cena? In Italy, a night out on the town begins with the Aperitivo (literally meaning a before-dinner drink to stimulate the appetite)…. the meaning has been transformed to include some FABULOUS buffet spreads that are usually “free” as long as you are consuming some pricey cocktails. It has become such a huge trend that people will treat the experience as a dinner in itself, filling up on all of the finger-friendly delicacies while having their drinks. Cena=Dinner. So the clever name “APERI-CENA” was born. Firenze is a huge city for “Aperi-cena”… with all of the magnificent outdoor spaces that give view to architectural treasures, it lends itself perfectly to casually wine, dine & just take in the surroundings. So we are bringing our own version of the “APERI-CENA” to Firenze a Tavola. Our team of Italian chefs will create quite the spread of Tuscan specialties and we will be pouring some great wines… $22 will give access to the buffet and reservations are highly recommended. This different approach to Community Table will have you mingling a bit more but with tables close by to sit and enjoy the food. We hope to offer the APERI-CENA on a regular basis and we will of course continue to have our traditional Community Table. $22/person excludes tax, gratuity & beverages Call 303.561.0234 for reservations or click the “Firenze a Tavola” link below & select “reservations”
